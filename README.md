# Memopad

Quick access to your notes and prompts, right in your sidebar.

## Features

**Organize with folders**
Create nested folders to keep your notes structured and easy to find.

**Drag and drop**
Move notes and folders around with simple drag and drop.

**Fast access**
All your notes are just one click away in the sidebar.

**Plain text storage**
Your notes live in `~/.memopad` as simple text files. No database, no lock-in.

## Getting Started

1. Click the Memopad icon in the activity bar
2. Use the `+` button to add notes or folders
3. Click any note to open and edit
4. Drag items to reorganize
5. Right-click for more options

## Storage

Notes are stored as plain text files in your home directory:
```
~/.memopad/
  work/
    meeting-notes.md
    code-review.md
  personal/
    ideas.txt
```
